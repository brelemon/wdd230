Discover Pleasant Grove

You visited this page days ago.

Home of the vikings and Strawberry Days

Find year round fun in Pleasant Grove.

City clock

The city clock tower is across the street from Water Gardens Cinema, and just down the street from Main Street.

Pleasant Grove is a city of history

Visit Old Pleasant Grove Fort, one of many historic buildings in Pleasant Grove.

Annual Strawberry Days Fair

Enjoy the annual Strawberry Days fair which takes place every year within the second week of June.

Battle Creek Falls

Stretch your legs and connect with nature. Battle Creek Falls is an easy 20 minute hike to a beautiful waterfall.

Beautiful hikes

If you love to take in the scenery there are many different hikes within the area.

Enjoy crossfit at the PG rec-center!

The Pleasant Grove Rec Center has everything you need. From pickelball courts, a weight room, a crossfit area, a track and more.

Winter Runs

Don't let winter keep you from enjoying a run outside.

Bike Path

During the summer enjoy miles and miles of a beautiful bike path that can take you to Provo Canyon, or up to South Jordan.
