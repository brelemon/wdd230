Target Audience

This site is for business owners to add information about their business. So that members of the community can have a directory of businesses and events going on in the local city. It's a great resource for residents and those who are visiting the city. Deomgraphics: men and women ages 30+.

Persona 1

Emily is 32 and owns and operates a cake business in Pleasant Grove. She has had her business for 10+ years and would be an ideal member for the chamber of commerce website.

She cares about the community and often speaks out about causes she cares about, does charity work and often promotes sales on her social media.

Persona 2

Linda is 38 and is a member of the PTA at her kid's high school. She is an active and involved member of the community. She is the go to person for information on events and latest and great hot spots to eat out at. She has a her degree in family studies and enjoys going on hikes with her family.
